Our Story

Founded in 2015, Cozy Coffee began with a simple mission: to create a warm, welcoming space where quality coffee meets community. What started as a small corner café has grown into a beloved local institution, serving carefully crafted beverages and fostering meaningful connections.

Our passion for coffee goes beyond just serving drinks - we're dedicated to sourcing the finest beans, supporting local farmers, and creating sustainable relationships throughout our supply chain.
